Why Crystal?

When you learn a new language, you want to be able to actually use it. With real people. In the real world. Crystal generally, is designed with that goal in mind, to help you move past the basics and become fluent.

Comprehensive content

Intuitive Language Construction with realistic scenarios designed by over 100 linguists which builds the four conversational skills necessary to establish meaningful connections in a new language: vocabulary, pronunciation, grammar, and culture.

Learn anywhere, any device

Compatibility with most devices, with lessons that are only 10-15 min.

Remember everything

The Crystal platform is designed to introduce new words and phrases at various intervals, through memory-building and critical-thinking exercises, and activities built to retain what you've learned
